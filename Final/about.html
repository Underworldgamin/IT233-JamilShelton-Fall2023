About Me

Hi, I'm Jamil Shelton

I am a passionate and family-oriented web developer with a love for creating meaningful digital experiences. My journey in programming began when I discovered the power of turning ideas into functional and beautiful websites.

With a background in Computer Science, I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with exploring frameworks like React.js and Node.js. I enjoy the challenge of solving problems and the creativity involved in crafting elegant solutions.

Outside of coding, I am a dedicated family person. I believe in the importance of work-life balance and cherish moments spent with my loved ones. Being family-oriented is not just a value in my personal life, but it influences my approach to collaboration and teamwork in professional settings.

I am always eager to learn, grow, and contribute to projects that have a positive impact. Whether it's creating intuitive user interfaces or solving complex technical challenges, I am committed to delivering high-quality results.
